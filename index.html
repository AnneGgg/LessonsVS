When you get a choice

There are so many different things to try in our big wide world. Sometimes it is easier to start with something you think is achievable. With a comfort zone in education, it was easy to choose to learn something new. And with a huge lack of knowledge in technology, I am so grateful to have found an option to learn and increase my skills.

Choose something that:

  • Excites you
  • You are curious about
  • You are ready to try

All of the skills I am learning are both challenging and exciting. Ideas have started to form that I look forward to trying. It is so hard to believe that in a few short months I went from someone who knew very little about technology, to this person who is gaining confidence in the ability to tackle different tasks.

When there is no choice

Sometimes there is no choice, and a new experience is thrust on you, if it is exciting, then it is easier to lean into the experience. If the experience is scary, with a lot of unknowns, then try your best to stay calm, pay attention, and trust that you will get through the experience feeling satisified with the outcome, or simply thankful that the experience is over.

  1. Trust yourself
  2. Rely on your support people
  3. Stay calm, and add your healthy stress relievers
  4. Take it one step at a time
  5. Believe you can do it

When all else fails, play a song that makes you happy.
